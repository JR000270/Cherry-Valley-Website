About Us

Who We Are

Friends of Cherry Valley is a 501c3 non-profit organization formed by concerned residents of the Valley in 2002.
Our mission is to conserve and enhance the natural and cultural resources, rural character and scenic beauty of the Cherry Valley area.
Visit our page on the National Wildlife Refuge System:
Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge | U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service (fws.gov)
Our Goals

• To improve connections and communications with homeowners, landowners and the community in general regarding conservation
and land preservation.
• To protect open space and environmentally sensitive areas.
• To strengthen our Board to prepare future leaders.
• To provide support to and community involvement in the Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge.
Our Mission Statement

The Friends of Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge are dedicated to the conservation and enhancement of the natural and cultural resources,
rural character and scenic beauty of the Cherry Valley area.
